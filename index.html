About me

My name is Ace King. I am wonux at Github and @孤逐王 at Sina Weibo. You can also reach me via email.

I am a communication engineer working on China Unicom which is a telecommunications company. After work I am a Linux amateur. The first time I used Linux in college，I fell in love with it. As the famous saying goes，"where there is shell,there is a way". It is really cool to work with the command line in the terminal. I Like Unix philosophy and opon source software. I agree with what DistroWatch said "Put the fun back into computing.Use Linux,BSD."

My favourite Linux distribution is gentoo,as well as arch. And I also dabble in C ,C++ and Python. A good developer is receptive and has the ability to learn new technologies. I also often contribute to open source projects and beta test startup products.
